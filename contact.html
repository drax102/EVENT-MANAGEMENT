Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our event planning services

How far in advance should I book your services?

We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance for major events like weddings and corporate functions. For smaller events, 1-2 months is typically sufficient. However, we can accommodate rush requests when our schedule permits.

What types of events do you specialize in?

We specialize in a wide range of events including corporate conferences, weddings, birthday parties, concerts, social gatherings, and themed events. Our experienced team can handle events of any size from 50 to 10,000+ guests.

Do you offer package deals or customized services?

We offer both pre-designed packages and fully customized services. Our packages provide great value for common event types, while our custom services allow you to tailor every aspect to your specific needs and budget.

What is included in your event planning services?

Our comprehensive services include venue selection, catering coordination, decoration design, entertainment booking, timeline management, vendor coordination, and on-site event supervision. We can provide as much or as little support as you need.

How do you handle event budgets?

We work with budgets of all sizes and provide transparent pricing. During our initial consultation, we'll discuss your budget and create a detailed proposal showing exactly where your investment goes. We always strive to maximize value while staying within your budget.
